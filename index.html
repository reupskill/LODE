What We Do

A complete finance function — from day-to-day operations to high-level strategic guidance.

Six integrated service lines designed to work independently or as a fully embedded partnership. Every service is built around one goal: moving your business forward.

01

Financial Operations Management

We take full ownership of your day-to-day financial operations — functioning as an embedded finance team without the overhead of a permanent hire.

What's Included
  • Accounts Payable & Receivable — end-to-end management with real-time visibility
  • Payroll Processing — accurate, timely, and jurisdiction-compliant payroll
  • Bank Reconciliation — regular reconciliation across all accounts
  • Bookkeeping & General Ledger — consistently maintained records
  • Month-End & Year-End Close — structured close processes, every period
  • Vendor & Expense Management — all expenditure tracked against budget
What You Gain

A clean, compliant, and current financial operation that runs without your direct involvement — giving you back time and complete confidence in your numbers.

Best suited for: Startups, SMEs, and growing businesses that need the output of a full finance function without the fixed overhead of a permanent team.

02

CFO & Strategic Advisory

The strategic financial guidance of a seasoned CFO without the cost of a full-time executive hire. High-level thinking grounded in your actual numbers.

What's Included
  • Financial Strategy Development — roadmap aligned with your business objectives
  • Board & Investor Reporting — performance narratives for key stakeholders
  • Capital Allocation Advisory — where to deploy capital for highest return
  • Pricing & Margin Strategy — optimise margins without losing competitiveness
  • Scenario Planning & Sensitivity Analysis — decisions under uncertainty, made clearly
  • Leadership Financial Coaching — building financial literacy at the top
What You Gain

A finance function that doesn't just report on the past — it actively shapes the future. Leadership with the insight to move decisively, at every critical moment.

Best suited for: Growing businesses with operational finance covered but lacking senior-level strategic financial leadership to drive major decisions.

03

Financial Reporting & Insights

Reports that do more than satisfy an obligation. Every deliverable is designed to be read, understood, and acted upon by the people running your business.

What's Included
  • Monthly Management Accounts — income, balance sheet, cash flow with commentary
  • Custom KPI Dashboards — real-time metrics your business actually needs to track
  • Variance Analysis — actual vs budget with clear explanations
  • Cash Flow Reporting & Forecasting — rolling visibility on runway at all times
  • Consolidated Group Reporting — clean accounts across multiple entities
  • Annual Financial Statements — statutory accounts in full compliance
What You Gain

Financial reports that tell the truth about your business — and give you the understanding to act on that truth with speed and confidence.

Best suited for: Businesses that have outgrown basic bookkeeping and need reporting that genuinely informs leadership decisions.

04

Compliance & Financial Governance

Your business stays financially compliant, well-governed, and built to withstand scrutiny — from regulators, investors, or auditors — at all times.

What's Included
  • Tax Planning & Filing — proactive management across all applicable jurisdictions
  • Regulatory Reporting — statutory filings prepared and submitted on time
  • Internal Controls Design — frameworks that prevent errors and reduce fraud risk
  • Audit Preparation & Support — books and documentation audit-ready
  • Financial Policy Development — policies and procedures for a well-run organisation
  • Multi-Jurisdiction Compliance — managing obligations across multiple countries
What You Gain

Organised, compliant, and audit-ready at all times — protecting your reputation and enabling you to operate in any market with confidence.

Best suited for: Businesses expanding into new markets, those approaching audits, and companies whose compliance position is not where it should be.

05

Investment Readiness

We prepare businesses for the scrutiny that comes with raising capital — making sure your finances tell the right story before any investor ever sees them.

What's Included
  • Investor-Grade Financial Models — with clear assumptions and scenario analysis
  • Data Room Preparation — structured and populated with what investors expect
  • Due Diligence Readiness — identify and resolve issues before they become deal-blockers
  • Financial Narrative Development — numbers translated into a compelling investor story
  • Valuation Support — financial foundation for a credible, defensible valuation
  • Post-Investment Integration — reporting and governance structures investors expect
What You Gain

Walk into any investor conversation with complete financial confidence — and walk out with the outcome your business deserves.

Best suited for: Businesses approaching seed, Series A/B, private equity, or debt financing — and those preparing for strategic exits.

06

Growth & Profitability Planning

The financial roadmap for your next phase — ensuring that growth is intentional, measured, and sustainable from day one.

What's Included
  • Annual Budgeting & Financial Planning — targets grounded in data, not aspiration
  • Rolling Forecasts — always-current view of where you are headed
  • Unit Economics Analysis — understand the true cost and return of every unit of growth
  • Profitability Optimisation — identify where margin is being lost and recover it
  • Cost Reduction Strategy — systematic savings without compromising growth pace
  • Revenue Expansion Modelling — the most financially efficient path to scale
What You Gain

A growth plan grounded in reality — the clarity to invest boldly and the discipline to grow profitably without outrunning your financial infrastructure.

Best suited for: Scaling businesses entering their next phase of growth who need financial rigour to match their commercial ambition.

How We Work

A structured process built for momentum, not bureaucracy.

From first conversation to full delivery — in as little as two weeks. Here is exactly what working with LODE. looks like.

Every LODE. engagement follows a proven onboarding process — designed to make the transition smooth, fast, and disruption-free. We take ownership of the complexity so you do not have to.

01

Discovery Conversation

We begin with a focused conversation about your business — where it is, where you want it to go, and what your current financial situation looks like. There is no template for this. We listen, ask the questions most people do not ask, and build a clear picture of what you actually need before we recommend anything.

02

Financial Assessment

Before we propose anything, we assess. We review your current financial records, processes, systems, and reporting to understand the gaps, risks, and opportunities. This assessment forms the factual basis for everything we recommend — so our proposed scope is always grounded in the reality of your business, not a generic service menu.

03

Engagement Design

We design a tailored engagement scope based on what your business specifically needs. This includes the services we will deliver, the cadence of delivery, the communication structure, and the key outcomes we are working toward. You review, refine, and approve. Nothing starts until we are fully aligned.

04

Onboarding & Integration

We integrate into your business efficiently and with minimal disruption — gaining access to your financial systems, meeting relevant team members, migrating historical data where required, and setting up the tools, templates, and workflows we will use going forward. Most clients are fully onboarded within two weeks.

05

Execution & Delivery

We deliver. Your financial operations run on schedule, your reports arrive when they should, your compliance is managed proactively, and your advisory input is available when decisions need to be made. You have a dedicated point of contact at all times — someone who knows your business, not a rotating support queue.

06

Review & Continuous Improvement

We run structured quarterly reviews with every client — assessing what is working, what has changed, and where our engagement should evolve. As your business grows, LODE. grows with it. Our goal is not to deliver a fixed scope indefinitely — it is to always be working on what your business needs most, right now.

We move quickly. Most clients are fully operational with LODE. within two weeks of signing — from first conversation to first deliverable.

  • Financial strategy development and scenario planning

Project-Based Engagement

Hybrid Model

For businesses in transition — scaling, entering new markets, or rebuilding

  • Selective outsourcing of specific finance operations
  • Advisory support layered on top of your in-house team
  • Flexible scope that expands or contracts with your needs
  • Structured handover planning toward future in-house hire
  • Works alongside existing finance staff without duplication
  • Best for: Businesses in transition — scaling up, entering new markets, or rebuilding their finance function
